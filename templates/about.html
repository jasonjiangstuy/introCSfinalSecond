About

Name: Homework Cleaner
Authors: Jason Jiang and Ethan Shan
What it does: Cleans images of worksheets by removing handwriting

Diffrences with Project Proposal:
  1. Does not do a perfect job, and can leave bits of handwriting on the image/ cut off bits of typed words
  2. Requires User to submit background color
Problems/It would be best to not submit:
  1. User should scan the image, to make sure the page is exactly/ close to perfectly vertical
  2. Fonts where the words are connected/overlap each other
  3. Short Typed Sentences
  4. animated gif
  5. Super neat handwriting
  6. Tiny Font
