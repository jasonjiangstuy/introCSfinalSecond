How To Use Our HandWriter Eraser

  1. Using a marker in a color other than black, draw a line exactly on the edge of the paper from the top right, to about a tenth of the way down
  2. Make sure the paper is right side up
  3. Take the picture as straight as you can
  4. Upload the file, and press next
  5. Use the dropper to give us the color of the marker that you've marked the top left corner with
  6. (Optionally) In the next step change the default variables that will be used to prossess the images
  7. Finally, look at the final image, you can choose to re process it with diffrent variables, or manually white out the image

What is allowed:

  1. all sizes of paper and oriantation (landscape is acceptable)

What will affect our preformance:

  1. Fonts where the words are connected/overlap each other
  2. Short Typed Sentances
  3. Slanted typed words (ex. bad printing job)
